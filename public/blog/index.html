Welcome to My Tech Blog

Welcome to my personal corner of the internet. This blog is a place where I document things I learn, build, break, and investigate.

Most posts will focus on topics such as:

  • Cybersecurity and SOC investigations
  • Microsoft Sentinel, Defender, and KQL hunting
  • Home lab projects and infrastructure
  • Networking and self-hosting
  • Random technical experiments

The goal is simple: write things down so they are useful later, both for myself and for others who run into the same problems. As well as develop my skills deeper, I found that when I do a write up I tend to go 2-3 layers deeper and thus learn a lot more (duh).
